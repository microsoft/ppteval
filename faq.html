What domain does PPTEval benchmark?

GUI-based slide editing and creation in PowerPoint Online—requiring multimodal understanding and precise manipulation beyond static text generation.

How many tasks are included?

120 tasks across 12 files covering diverse intents and difficulty levels.

Why use rubric scoring?

Tasks often admit multiple valid solutions and agents make partial progress; rubrics award fractional credit and give natural-language feedback.

What metrics should I focus on?

Success Rate (perfect completions) and Average Partial Score (mean rubric score) jointly reveal capability and incremental progress.

How are scores aggregated?

A modified formula balances progress on critical checkpoints with penalties from non-critical issues—avoiding harsh zeroing of near-complete attempts.

What are common failure modes?

Misplaced objects, incomplete multi-element diagram updates, unintended animations, and destructive global changes.
